The Singapore Flag

The Flag of Singapore (CSS Flag 2025)

The flag of Singapore consists of two horizontal bands: red on top and white on the bottom.

On the red band, there is a white crescent moon and five white stars arranged in a circle.

Symbolism:

  • Red: Universal brotherhood and equality of man
  • White: Pervading and everlasting purity and virtue
  • Crescent Moon: A young nation on the rise
  • Five Stars: Democracy, peace, progress, justice, and equality
